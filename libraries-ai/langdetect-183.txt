To download the pre-built model used in OpenNLPLanguageDetector.java, follow these steps:
1. Go to https://downloads.apache.org/opennlp/models/langdetect/1.8.3/ and click on the link langdetect-183.bin.
2. Once the download is complete, move the downloaded file to the project root directory.
3. Update the assert statement to assertEquals("eng", language.getLang());
